# Secret Warfare

In a world of secrets and shadows, only the unseen survive. As an elite agent in the Phantom Strike Division, you'll infiltrate, sabotage, and vanish without a trace. With stealth-driven levels and high-tech gear, every mission is a test of precision and nerve. Are you ready to step into the shadows and rewrite the rules of espionage?

**Official Website:** https://sullydux.github.io/Secret-Warfare/

**Developer:** [Sullydux](https://github.com/sullydux)

---

## How to Get Started

Read the intro message, if not your first time it will skip that, then the game will start automaticly. In that menu you can pick levels, change to mobile or computer, restart game or change volume. When you open a level it will give a mission brief that you can skip then the level will start. 

Notice: level one has reports of being complicated, so I'd recomend paper or to skip it.
